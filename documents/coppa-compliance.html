COPPA Compliance Statement

Effective Date: June 20, 2025

Last Updated: June 20, 2025

Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

SpellingBud is committed to protecting the privacy of children under 13 years of age. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and have implemented specific measures to ensure children's safety while using our educational app.

COPPA Compliance Measures

Age Verification

  • Mandatory Age Screen: All users must verify their age before accessing the app
  • Clear Age Ranges: Users select "Under 13" or "13 years or older"
  • One-Time Verification: Age verification is required only once and stored locally

Data Collection from Children Under 13

Information We DO Collect:

  • Age Range Only: Whether the user is under 13 or 13+ (no specific birth dates)
  • Learning Progress: Spelling practice progress stored locally on the device
  • App Usage: Basic functionality usage for app improvement (anonymized)

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • No Personal Identifiers: Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or addresses
  • No Photos or Videos: No camera or media access
  • No Location Data: No GPS or location tracking
  • No Social Information: No social media integration or sharing
  • No Behavioral Profiling: No tracking of browsing habits or interests outside the app

Parental Rights Under COPPA

Parents have the right to:

  • Review Information: Request to see any information we have collected about their child
  • Delete Information: Request deletion of their child's information
  • Refuse Collection: Refuse to allow further collection of their child's information
  • Control Disclosure: Control whether their child's information is disclosed to third parties

How to Exercise Parental Rights

Contact Information:

  • Email: spellingbud@googlegroups.com
  • Subject Line: "COPPA Parental Rights Request"
  • Response Time: Within 48 hours

Required Information for Requests:

  • Parent/guardian name and contact information
  • Child's age range (as entered in the app)
  • Specific request (review, delete, or refuse collection)
  • Verification of parental status may be required

Third-Party Services and Children

Google AdMob (Advertising)

  • Child-Safe Ads: Only family-friendly, educational advertisements shown to children
  • No Behavioral Targeting: Ads are not based on child's personal information or behavior
  • COPPA-Compliant: AdMob is configured for child-directed treatment
  • Limited Data: Only basic device information for ad delivery (no personal data)

Google Play Services

  • No Direct Interaction: Children do not interact directly with Google Play services
  • Parent-Controlled: Any premium purchases require parental approval through device settings
  • Secure Processing: Payment information is handled by Google Play, not our app

Data Security for Children

Local Storage Only

  • Device-Based: All child learning data stored locally on the device
  • No Cloud Sync: Children's learning progress is not uploaded to external servers
  • Secure Storage: Data encrypted using Android's built-in security measures

No Data Transmission

  • Age Information: Never transmitted outside the device
  • Learning Progress: Stays on the local device only
  • No Analytics: No personal analytics data sent for children under 13

Educational Content Safety

Age-Appropriate Content

  • Educational Focus: All content designed for learning and skill development
  • Family-Friendly: No inappropriate language, images, or themes
  • Curriculum-Aligned: Content based on standard educational spelling curricula

Safe Learning Environment

  • No Social Features: No chat, messaging, or social interaction capabilities
  • No External Links: No links to external websites or content
  • Offline Capable: Can be used without internet connection for added safety

Compliance Monitoring

Regular Reviews

  • Privacy Audits: Regular review of data practices
  • Content Review: Ongoing evaluation of educational content appropriateness
  • Third-Party Assessment: Periodic COPPA compliance assessments

Staff Training

  • Privacy Training: All staff trained on COPPA requirements
  • Child Safety: Team educated on children's digital safety best practices
  • Legal Updates: Regular updates on children's privacy law changes

Contact for COPPA Concerns

Primary Contact:

  • Email: spellingbud@googlegroups.com

Emergency Contact (for urgent privacy concerns):

  • Email: spellingbud@googlegroups.com
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours

Safe Harbor Provisions

We rely on the following safe harbor provisions under COPPA:

  • Educational Purpose: App is designed solely for educational use
  • Minimal Collection: We collect only information necessary for the educational service
  • No Disclosure: We do not disclose children's personal information to third parties
  • Parental Control: Parents have full control over their child's information

Reporting COPPA Violations

If you believe we have violated COPPA or have concerns about children's privacy:

  1. Contact Us First: Email spellingbud@googlegroups.com with your concerns
  2. FTC Reporting: You may also report concerns to the Federal Trade Commission
  3. State Authorities: Contact your state's attorney general office if applicable

Updates to COPPA Compliance

We will notify parents of any material changes to our COPPA practices through:

  • Email Notification: Where contact information is available
  • In-App Notice: Prominent notice within the application
  • Website Update: Updated information on our privacy policy page
